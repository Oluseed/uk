Description

It is now much easier for individuals and corporate bodies who make use of the Waste disposal services of various State Waste Management Authorities to pay for these services.

Features
  • Streamlined bills payment process in-branch that makes paying Waste bills a breeze.
Benefits
  • Ease of payment as a result of proximity of bank branches to customers.
  • No extra charge on bills paid in-branch.
Required Documents
  • Customer’s Waste Bills.

Does FirstBank collect Waste Bills for my State?

At the moment, FirstBank only collects waste bills in Lagos, Edo and Enugu. Other locations are being steadily rolled out.
